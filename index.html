Koki Satsukawa, Ph.D.

Koki Satsukawa is an Associate professor/lecturer at the Institute of Transdisciplinary Sciences for Innovation, Kanazawa University. He received a Doctor of Engineering in Civil Engineering from the University of Tokyo in March 2019. His research focuses on traffic flow theory, network traffic assignment, and traffic management/control. Specific areas include dynamic network flow analysis (e.g. equilibrium properties in dynamic traffic assignment, macroscopic performance of urban traffic flows) and the development of dynamic control strategies (e.g. congestion pricing, signal control).
