Koki Satsukawa, Associate professor/lecturer at Kanazawa University.

Welcome

Koki Satsukawa is an Associate professor/lecturer at College of Transdisciplinary Sciences for Innovation at Kanazawa University. He received the degree of Doctor of Engineering in the field of Civil Engineering from the University of Tokyo on March 2019. His major research interests are traffic flow theory, network traffic assignment and traffic management/control. Specific areas of research include the analysis of dynamic network flow (e.g. properties of equilibrium in dynamic traffic assignment, macroscopic performance of urban traffic flows) and the development of dynamic control strategies (e.g. congestion pricing, signal control).
